AR Heritage Trails

Little India Trail

Known as Serangoon before 1980s, the Little India precint melds the old with the new and hosts trades from the past alongside modern businesses

Peranakan Trail

Peranakan culture in Singapore has roots tracing all the way back to Sir Stamford Raffles’ arrival in 1819.

Kampong Glam Trail

Kampong Glam got its name from the Malay word gelam (cajeput tree) which grew locally and has many uses. Its bark can be used for weaving and to caulk boats

Chinatown Trail

Chinatown, also known as 牛车水 Niu Che Shui, or bullock water cart in Mandarin, was named after the ox-drawn carts that transported water to the area.
